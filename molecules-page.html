Combinations of Atoms

Molecules are simple combinations of atoms working together to form functional units. In eLearning, this might be a form element composed of a radio button and its label, a progress bar with text, or a tooltip with an icon. These small, reusable groupings enable consistent UI behaviour and are often used across question sets, interactions, and navigation elements such as tooltips or modals.
